Week 9

Extent of Chinese Economic Influence in Africa vs Is Influence Positive or Negative

This chart is a scatterplot of the mean answers, by country, to a survey conducted by Afrobarometer. Sample size for each country is shown by the bubble size and an orange gradient scale; the larger the sample size, the darker the shade of orange. The 'Positive/Negative' score is regressed on the 'Extent of Economic Influence' score, producing a linear regression line and an R^2 value of 0.46. A tooltip is added so readers can see the Country pertaining to each bubble, and the actual values of x and y.
